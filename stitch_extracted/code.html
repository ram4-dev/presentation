Building with Intent

Software is a craft. I believe in small teams, high ownership, and the obsessive pursuit of simplicity. Complexity is the enemy of reliability.

01. Speed as a Feature

The fastest feedback loop wins. Ship today, refine tomorrow. Velocity is the only sustainable competitive advantage.

02. Ownership at Every Level

No silos. Every engineer should understand the product vision, and every product owner should understand the technical constraints.

03. Radical Simplicity

Choose boring technology for critical systems. Innovate on the product, not the database driver, unless absolutely necessary.
